Sign up with us, start helping others and earn money

We build a community of top notch developers that are willing to contribute from their knowledge to others, the main difference about Floatward is that we value your time and efforts, and we are paying you for that.

We are not replacing Stackoverflow and as such, we found out that 80% of developers spend 25% of their time solving problems. so, many problem could be solved by existing online resources, but when you have a tight schedule, and need to get immediate assistance, you would like someone on your side, and that is what we offer. On demand tool to solve very specific technological problems, easier and faster.

How it works

  • Sign up with us
  • Set your profile, or import it from Github or Stackoverflow
  • Let us know what are your Forte expertise, those your are confident to know much better than others, that you would like to consult on
  • Install our app and once you have time slot you feel like to helping others, set your status to online, and only then others can approach you with assistance requests.

Helping others

Once you are called by a colleague, you both will be connected using a Google Hangout session, so you could chat, talk, see each other, and obviously share desktops to go through code.
Since we are charging for this time, please try to be focused and efficient as possible.

Getting paid

You are getting paid for two things.
  • Session time - the actual time you have helped others, we charge by the minute, so we aggregate the total session time per month and deliver the money to your bank account
  • Online time - in some cases we’ll be offering a payment for people to be ready to help others, in order to make sure that there are enough experts out there that can solve the community members urgent issues. So, you’ll be paid just to be Online for a period of time
