Sign up with us, start helping others and earn money

We build a community of top notch developers that are willing to contribute from their knowledge to others,
The main difference about Floatward is that we are offering an on demand assistance, through online sessions. And we are paying for your time and efforts

We are not replacing your best practice of solving problems, but we found out that 80% of developers spend 25% of their time solving problems. so, when you have a tight schedule, or when you would like to have someone on your side, Floatward can help you do it easier and faster.

How it works

  • Sign up with us
  • Set your profile, or import it from Github or Stackoverflow
  • Let us know what are you expert in, and by expert we mean that in that specific field you are better than the other 99% in the field.
  • Use Floatward app It is totally up tp you when to go online, once you do, People in need can find you and ask for your help.

Helping others

Once you are called out by a colleague, you both will be connected through an online session, so you could chat, talk, see each other, and obviously share desktops to go through code.

Getting paid

You are getting paid for two things.
  • Session time - the actual time you have helped others, we charge by the minute, so we aggregate the total session time per month and deliver the money to your bank account
  • Online time - in some cases we’ll be offering a payment for people to be ready to help others, in order to make sure that there are enough experts out there to solve the community members urgent issues. So, you’ll be paid just to be Online for a period of time
